Anja Samsom

Anja is a software engineer at Versana in New York City. Previously, while a student at the University of Vermont studying Computer Science & Information Systems in the College of Engineering and Mathematical Sciences and the Honors College, Anja was the president of the Society of Women in Computer Science, an Admissions Representative, and an intern for the Vermont Space Grant / NASA EPSCoR. She did her Honors thesis on the Impact of Female Role Models on the Retention of Women in Computer Science, which can be found here. This website is a digital collections of her accomplishments, while also being a fun hobby to work on when time allows!
